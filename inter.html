Interior

Living Area

Experience refined comfort at Psusennes, where bold textures meet the serenity of nature. This sanctuary features a dramatic granite backdrop, an illuminated floating staircase, and a lush indoor garden that seamlessly connects grand architecture with organic tranquility. Every detail is crafted to deliver an extraordinary environment for relaxation.

Kitchen & Dining Area

The kitchen and dining area is a masterclass in modern functionality and bold aesthetics. Anchored by a black marble island and sculptural light installation, the space blends warm timber with sleek craftsmanship.

Bedroom

The master bedroom at Psusennes is a refined sanctuary where dramatic dark textures and warm timber flooring create a harmonious space for rest. Every element is carefully balanced to provide an extraordinary and tranquil escape.

Bathroom

The bathroom is a refined sanctuary where dramatic dark textures and warm timber flooring create a harmonious space for rest. From the illuminated feature wall to the floor-to-ceiling glass, every element enhances relaxation.
